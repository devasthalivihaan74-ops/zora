Boss Man

Eau De Parfum  ·  50 ML

ZORA Boss Man is a bold, boardroom-ready Eau De Parfum built for those who lead the room without saying a word. Rich amber and warm spice open the composition, settling into a clean, confident woody base that carries from the first meeting into the evening.

Designed for the man who is always on — presentations, dinners, first impressions and everything after — Boss Man gives a sharp, assured presence without ever feeling heavy. It's finished in a striking navy-and-gold bottle that looks as deliberate as the fragrance inside it.
